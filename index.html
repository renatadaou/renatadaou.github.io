The Amazon Faces a New Threat: Fire

Intensified agricultural burning pushes fires into virgin forests, worsening environmental crisis. The story highlights several critical issues related to land and fire management in the region. It identifies illegal land grabbing practices (grilagem) and underscores the severe impact of drought on both transportation infrastructure and wildlife. Firefighting operations, such as Operation Aceiro, face significant logistical and operational challenges. There are notable discrepancies in deforestation data and widespread underreporting of fire incidents. Satellite imagery analysis reveals fire hotspots frequently occurring within registered agricultural zones, while the government's fire management efforts and resource allocation often neglect remote and unregistered areas, where the most destructive fires take place.

Ripple Effect of the Syrian Refugee Crisis

The criminalization of humanitarian work has increased since the peak of migration out of Syria in 2016. EU member states' actions have left asylum-seeking immigrants isolated and devoid of assistance upon their arrival in Europe, following arduous and dangerous journeys. This project involved mapping the countries with the most immigrants from Syria after scraping data from UNHCR.
